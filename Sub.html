Sub Weapons

Sub weapons are usually bombs or disruptors of any kind in Splatoon 2. They have the purpose to either space out enemies or kill them directly, maybe only damage them. They have a high variety of ways to use them, but every bomb has its own unique properties, which is the topic of this site.

Top player Sorin goes over the sub weapons and ranks them in different tiers in this video. DISCLAIMER: This video is old and some aspects might be outdated.

A graph showing the effect of the ink saver sub ability over multiple ability points.

sub weapon name sprite/image ink consumption base/chip/far chip damage sub power up effect opinion on usage
Splat Bomb 70% 180/30 Increases air velocity and range The splat bomb is a fast exploding bomb that can land nasty kills on slow opponents or momentarily endanger opponents during fights. It should generally be played around spacing and pressuring opponents to either alleviate the difficulty of a bad matchup or grant more security in easier to win fights.
Suction Bomb 70% 220/30 Increases air velocity and range Suction bombs stick to anything you throw them at that isn't playermade or a player character. They take a really long time to explode once deployed and therefore can't lock enemies since they have enough time to escape anyway. It has a really big explosion radius, though, which makes it very useful in long fights and fights with multiple players, making the suction bomb a great support bomb. You can also exploit its hitbox by sticking them at corners. This will use the whole hitbox while the bomb is barely visible.
Burst Bomb 40% 60/35/25 Increases air velocity and range The burst bomb is the only damaging sub weapon that you can throw twice consecutively without any ink saver sub. Its damage is just high enough to kill with two direct hits making it useful for low damage weapons. The biggest factor, however, is that it explodes on impact immediately and paints a lot, in return giving a lot of mobility in the splash-area. It's strong for slayers in facto mobility, strong for supports to deal chip damage and strong for anchors to defend themself.
Autobomb 55% 180/30 Increases air velocity and range The autobomb slowly walks towards nearby opponents from where it lands. Due to this it is very easy to dodge and not really strong. However, its ability to track down enemies is extraordinary and its always surprising how many kills you get on opponents who attack after being tracked and forget about the autobomb following them. It definitely applies some pressure to the opponents but not quite effective enough to be really strong.
Curling Bomb 70% 180/30
20(contact)		 Increases ground velocity and range Curling bombs are being thrown on the ground and they roll after being deployed. They paint a straight line in the direction of deployment and therefore give a lot of mobility outside of fights. It is rare that it kills, therefore the movement options it gives are its only strong side and also only useful to slayers. Its 20 damage on contact can be combined very well
Ink Mine 60% 45/35 Increases damage radius and point sensor effects If you deploy the ink mine, it only disappears once an enemy steps on it or a new one is placed(in which case the previously placed is triggered). Once triggered, it explodes dealing damage and having the effect of a point sensor on the spot additionally.
Toxic Mist 60% - Increases air velocity and range Being one of two non-damaging sub weapons, the toxic mist isn't that popular. The toxic mist explodes on impact and places a sphere shaped fog that slows down enemies drastically and highers their ink consumption/blocks regeneration a lot once entered. This usually doesn't have a lot of impact though, mainly because the toxic mist only works if you outrange the poisoned opponent and therefore are less versatile than most other bombs.
Splash Wall 60% 30 Increases base HP and durability Throwing it right in front of you, a wall made from ink is blocking opponent shots and damaging the opponents who try to pass through it. The splash wall makes it easier to push forward or hinder an opponent from pushing forward. Since its size is not very high, you do have to play around it very cautiously. Enemy bombs explode on direct impact and can kill you rather easily potentially once they overcome it.
Point Sensor 45% - Increases air velocity and range The point sensor tracks enemies that are in a spheric ball and locates them for 8 seconds after that. It helps backliners very well since they have a high damage output but need to shoot from far away, making it harder to hit stealthy or cautious opponents.
Sprinkler 60% 20 increases duration of high-power and mid-power phase The sprinkler is a throwable sub weapon that spins and sprays ink all around. Once deployed it goes into high power mode and over time goes into mid power and low power mode. It only disappears once shot down by an enemy or a new one is placed. It's great to charge specials and paint a lot if placed on a ceiling of any kind that increases the effective circle.
Squid Beakon 75% - Increases super jump speed through adding quick super jump You can place up to 3 beakons and each of them can be used twice (unless you deployed the squid beakon, in which case it disappears completely). They help lessen the effect of deaths by far and using sub power up offer a fast escape option out of dangerous situations They should always be placed in safe spots that are hidden and hard to reach from the opponents.
Fizzy Bomb 60% 50/35 Increases air velocity and range The fizzy bomb explodes really fast uncharged, and can explode up to three times if charged. This makes this sub weapon the highest damaging out of all and is perfectly suit to shred obstacles. Its high damage makes it also very strong for slayers to combo with, such as the Kensa Sloshing Machine. Generally a very strong damaging sub weapon that's useful in many different ways.
Torpedo 65% 60/35/12 Increases general velocity and range Torpedos explode, similarly to Fizzy bombs, multiple times. If you throw it near in opponent in the air, it locks on them and flies directly on the locked spot. The opponent has to shoot it or else he is gonna take at least 35 and up to 84 damage. This is very strong distraction and makes killing much easier in 1v1s. It's also a super strong supporting sub weapon and paint a lot if it locks on someone. Once thrown on the ground, however, it explodes once and very fast dealing 35 damage making weapon that deal 65 damage per shot one-hit.
